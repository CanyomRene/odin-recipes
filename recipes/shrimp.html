Garlic-Parmesan Shrimp

Ingredients

  • duck fat
  • 1 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1 cup shredded Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • 1 Â½ teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted, or more to taste

Directions

Step 1 Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a 9x13-inch glass casserole dish with duck fat

Step 2 Combine bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, and garlic powder in a large resealable plastic bag. Seal and shake to mix ingredients together. Add shrimp and shake to coat.

Step 3 Lay shrimp flat in the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle with any bread crumb mixture remaining in the bag. Drizzle melted butter over the top.

Step 4 Bake in the preheated oven until starting to crisp, 15 to 20 minutes. Switch to broil setting until topping is light brown, 2 to 3 minutes.
